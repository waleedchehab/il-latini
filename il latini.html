location

The Latin District is located in a privileged geographical location in the new city of El Alamein (14 km along the north-coast road) near the heritage city, the universities and culture area, and the cinemas and theater complex, as well as its proximity to El Alamein International Airport, In addition, The Latin District is located near the main roads and axes of the New Alamein City, such as Alexandria Matrouh Road and New Fouka Road. The Latin District is considered one of the most distinguished Districts in the Greek and Roman tourist city of El Alamein.

services

The Latin District in New Alamein is characterized by a distinguished group of different services and public facilities that the owner company has taken care of to suit all customers and meet all life requirements and from these services:

  • The International University for Science and Technology and international schools in New Alamein
  • A huge library similar to Alexandria’s Library
  • Hotel area and towers similar to Dubai’s towers
  • Natural lakes and waterfalls.
  • Restaurants, cafes and cinema complex.
  • Track for running, walking and cycling.
  • An integrated health club & Gym halls and sports fields.
  • Surveillance cameras, guards, and security personnel.
  • Marsa yachting and fishing.
